I'm Shehan Reshin
My earliest encounters with computers were when I found myself playing Dangerous Dave for hours, enthralled in the magic of how it all worked. This sparked a passion within me to continually learn and explore. More than a decade and a couple of successful SQL injection attacks (legally) later, I found myself starting out my academic journey in cyber security. During that time, I found a love for creating software. There was just something so beautiful about building something from the ground up that I can never put into words. My primary goal is to build creative and sophisticated software whilst maintaining a seamless user experience.
